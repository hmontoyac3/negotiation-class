Your Situation
  • You're going to do a 3-month exchange program (April, May, June) and need to sublet your apartment or you'll be paying rent for nothing
  • Your apartment: furnished 2BR, good neighborhood, 15 min from campus by foot
  • Your current rent: €1,100/month — you need to at least cover this
  • Your BATNA: put it on Airbnb (could earn €1,600/month, but it's unpredictable — lots of turnover, cleaning costs, risk of bad guests)
  • You have one interested person — your partner in this negotiation
Your Numbers — Keep These Secret
€1,400 Your Target Covers rent + utilities + a bit extra
€1,100 Your Minimum Below this, Airbnb is less risky
What Matters Beyond Price
  • You care a lot about finding someone responsible — the place has your stuff in it
  • You'd accept €1,150 for someone who seems genuinely reliable and respectful
  • You're flexible on exact move-in date (anywhere in late March to April 1)
  • You'd appreciate someone who agrees to no parties and pays on time
⚠ Do Not Reveal
  • That your minimum is €1,100 — if they know, that's exactly what they'll offer
  • That you're not fully confident in Airbnb (turnover, guests, cleaning) — this weakens your BATNA
  • That you're a bit anxious about leaving the apartment unattended
