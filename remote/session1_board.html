THE DEBRIEF
Ask 2–3 pairs to share their outcome before starting the questions. Let the class react before you explain anything.
LEVEL 1 — What happened?
What price did you agree on? Who made the first offer?
Did anyone walk away without a deal? What made you decide that?
Did anyone reveal a number they wish they hadn't?
LEVEL 2 — Why?
What did AI tell you to do? Did it help? What did it miss?
Why does the first number said have so much influence on the final outcome?
What was your BATNA? Did knowing it change how you negotiated?
Did anyone trade non-monetary things (reliability, move-in date) to close the gap?
LEVEL 3 — So what?
You'll negotiate a lease, a salary, a freelance rate in the next 2 years. What's the one thing you'll do differently after today?
What would have been different if you'd prepared for 20 extra minutes?
💡 Key concept to land: BATNA = your real power. Anchoring = the first number shapes everything. Reservation price = sacred — never reveal it, never cross it. All three were present in this negotiation whether students named them or not.
