Session 1 · The Offer · Your Role

THE
CANDIDATE

You have a job offer from Nexus.
Now you need to negotiate the salary.
Your Situation
  • You've just received a written job offer from Nexus, a fast-growing B2B SaaS startup in Milan
  • The role is a Business Analyst position — a strong fit for your profile
  • You're graduating in June and this would be your first full-time job
  • HR told you the offer is "around €2,200/month" but said there's flexibility
  • You have one other option: a consulting firm called you back, but it's not a firm offer — it's uncertain, at least 6 weeks away, and you're not sure how much it would pay
Your Numbers — Keep These Secret
€2,800 Your Target What you're hoping to get
€2,500 Your Minimum Below this, you wait for the consulting callback
What You Also Care About (beyond salary)

You'd like to work from home 2 days per week — your commute is long. You'd also prefer to start in October rather than September, because you have a summer trip planned. These aren't dealbreakers, but they matter. The right combination of salary + flexibility might be worth more to you than a higher number alone.

⚠ Do Not Reveal
  • That your minimum is €2,500 — if they know it, they'll stop there
  • That the consulting offer is uncertain and 6 weeks away — this weakens your position
  • That you really want this job — it gives them less reason to move
