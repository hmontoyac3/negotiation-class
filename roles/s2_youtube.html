  • You and PodcastOne are the two finalists — you need to negotiate who gets it and at what price

Your Budget — Keep This Secret

Critical — What Your Platform Actually Needs

VisualSport is a video-only platform. You have no podcast feed, no audio editing team, and no audio distribution. What you need from this interview is the video footage — to edit into a 20-minute YouTube feature. The audio-only recording is completely useless to you. You would never use it. But you haven't thought to mention this, because you assumed the other side wants the same thing you do.

⚠ Do Not Reveal
  • Your budget ceiling of €90,000
  • That the Wimbledon documentary is an option — shows you have an alternative and weakens their leverage
